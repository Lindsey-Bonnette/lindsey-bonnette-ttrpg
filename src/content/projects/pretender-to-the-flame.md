A volcano sits at the edge of eruption and ash coats the forest as a parasite puppets the fledgling volcano god that could control it all. Venture into the Reisen forest during this perilous time. Locate missing people, help evacuation efforts, solve a spreading disease, or maybe prevent a volcanic eruption and the political machinations behind it.

Pretender to the Flame is an adventure kit written with Worlds Without Number (WWN) RPG in mind, though it is easily converted to other OSR systems. This kit allows you to quickly design your own adventure to fit any level or size of adventuring party. Match the adventure to your play group's preferred style and easily use only the bits you like. The kit is a 24 5x8 page booklet with all the locations, antagonists, and situations you need to run a short campaign in this ash covered forest. Slot this kit into an existing sandbox, make it a contained campaign, or just take pieces the choice is yours.
