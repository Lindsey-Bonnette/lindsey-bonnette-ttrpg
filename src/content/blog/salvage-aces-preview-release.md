Hello! I'm popping in to share what I've been working on. A set of quick start preview rules. I say preview, as the final element, a starter adventure, is not complete yet. However these rules are all that is required to play Salvage Aces in its most basic form.

Much of this information is a condensed version of the preview I released previously, but some things are new. I also gave myself some goals for the project going forward.

- Have a starter adventure done and play test the game for Halloween 2022
- Complete a full draft of the rules by the end of 2022
- Release the game Itch.io and as POD through DriveThru by June 2023

Finally a reminder that many of my posts are available a week early over on Patreon.
