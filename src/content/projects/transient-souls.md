Who cares for the dead when the reaper is away?

Transient Souls is a Push-Powered rpg that is built to provide an engaging narrative game with lightweight, fiction-first gameplay. Characters will find themselves traveling through surrealist landscapes, delving into dangerous locales for hidden knowledge, and experiencing their deathless world. Characters will be blessed by the fates in their quest, gaining fate bending abilities over time.
