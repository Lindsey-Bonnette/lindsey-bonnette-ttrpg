Welcome to Haunted Trappings. This product is a generator to create a themed haunted location vaguely set in the 1920's and beyond. Haunted Trappings does this by combining descriptors, locations, and a paranormal inhabitant to create haunts such as "Art deco civic building on the lakeshore haunted by a revenge seeking poltergeist."

To use the generator you will need one twelve-sided die, one eight-sided die, one to four six-sided dice with pips, and a note-taking tool.

The product is available for free as a PDF on Itch.io.
