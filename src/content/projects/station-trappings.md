INTRODUCTION

Station Trappings is a space station generator. A series of dice rolls on random tables provides a generalized theme and location such as, "Infested Research Station within an Asteroid Belt" as well as a full layout and room themes.

STEP BY STEP

Roll on the Station Purpose Table to determine a general purpose for the station
Roll on the Trappings Table to determine a theme for the station, this combines with the purpose to create the theme for the station
Roll on the Location Table to determine a physical location for the station
Determine the Station Room Layout
Roll on the Room Table and the Descriptor Table for each room of the station
Roll to determine the type of challenge present in each room

REQUIRED MATERIALS

One twelve-sided die
One eight-sided die
One to four six sided dice (with pips rather than numbers)
Note taking device
Paper to doodle (optional)
