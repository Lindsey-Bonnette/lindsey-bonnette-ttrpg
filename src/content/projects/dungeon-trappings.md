This dungeon generator will help create a themed dungeon, complete with challenges and adversaries using a series of dice rolls.

Dungeon Trappings does this by combining a generic location with a descriptor such as "Illusionary Catacombs". The generator digs further in, creating a quick dungeon layout and then giving a description to each room and challenge. The pdf includes all the tables needed for the generator and a full example of the system at work.

Required Materials:
- One twelve-sided die
- One eight-sided die
- One to four six-sided die (with pips rather than numbers)
- Note-taking device
- (Optional) Paper to doodle on
